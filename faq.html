Frequently asked questions

We use plant proteins, seaweed extracts, and natural flavors to mimic classic crab taste.

Do you offer subscriptions?

Yes, subscriptions are available for monthly delivery of your favorites.

Is the packaging recyclable?

Our boxes are curbside recyclable, and our insulation is reusable or recyclable.

Where are your products made?

We partner with coastal kitchens in the U.S. to craft small-batch runs.
